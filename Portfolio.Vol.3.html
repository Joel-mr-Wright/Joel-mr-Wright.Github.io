This portfolio showcases the work created during my time in practice at Neil Dowlman Architecture.



This portfolio showcases work such as drawings and renders created while working at Neil Dowlman Architecture as a Part 1. Also, there are some drawings towards the back of this portfolio of places visited during my time as a Part 1 Architects Assistant.

Much of this work was created with AutoCAD, SketchUp and Photoshop. During my time at Neil Dowlman Architecture, I became the main surveyor of the office, regularly going on a survey with laser distos and lidar systems. I worked on many projects involving surveying, drawing existing/proposed and towards the end, I was also working on technical drawings. There was also the opportunity to have a competition within the office to convert a disused agricultural shed (class Q). This competition was an opportunity to show my creativity with a rather simplistic building. The prize of winning this competition was the ability to build a model of the winning design.

I thank and appreciate you for the time taken to look through this portfolio.
